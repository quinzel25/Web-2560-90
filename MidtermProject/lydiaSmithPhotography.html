About Me
I have always loved photography. I remember paging through large photo books looking at all the fantastic images. Capturing these incredible places, people and moments. Pulling me into each photographic story. Currently I am a photographer and photo assistant in the Minneapolis area. I spend my time roller skating, hiking, collaborating with other artists and spending time with friends and family.
